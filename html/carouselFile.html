At the moment, it is unlikely that immediate action could be taken at a trade fair, but in some emergency cases , it is possible for IP holders to bring administrative or criminal proceedings at short notice.
Each country in South-East Asia have its own legal remedies , and most countries have a system to search for and seize infringing goods provided that there is very strong evidence of IP infringement.
In many countries such as Thailand, Indonesia or Vietnam, the court, authorities or police may refuse to seize the goods in the case of patent infringement as there may be complex issues involved. Trade marks or copyright infringement are more suitable for such an action.
